About Simone Guzzo

A specialist aesthetic nurse with over 10 years of clinical experience, dedicated to providing beauty with safety and natural results.

My Professional Journey

I am Simone Guzzo, a registered nurse with over a decade of experience in healthcare. My passion for nursing led me to specialise in aesthetics, where I found the perfect combination of clinical care and enhancing natural beauty.

I recently completed my third postgraduate degree in aesthetics, always seeking the most modern techniques and knowledge to offer the best care to my patients. After validating my qualifications in the United Kingdom, I am proud to be able to practice my profession with all the safety and quality my clients deserve.

Experience and Philosophy

During my years of clinical experience, I have developed a unique approach that combines advanced technical knowledge with an artistic eye for natural beauty. I believe that each person has unique characteristics that should be enhanced, not altered.

My philosophy is based on three fundamental pillars: clinical safety, natural results and humanised care. Each procedure is performed with the utmost care, using modern techniques and high-quality products.
