Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to your questions about aesthetic procedures, safety, and aftercare. Information based on clinical experience and scientific evidence.

Botox is one of the safest aesthetic procedures when performed by qualified professionals. As a specialist nurse, I follow strict safety protocols and only use products approved by the MHRA (UK).

Side effects are rare and usually mild, and may include:

  • Slight redness at the injection site
  • Minor bruising (rare)
  • Mild headache (temporary)

All risks are discussed during the consultation, and you will receive full guidance on post-procedure care.

Botox results typically last between 4 to 6 months, varying according to:

  • Individual metabolism
  • Treated area
  • Amount of product used
  • Frequency of previous treatments
