Therapeutic Botox

Botox is one of the safest and most effective procedures for treating expression lines. We use high-quality botulinum toxin to smooth out expression lines and prevent future wrinkles from deepening.

Forehead Wrinkles

Smoothing of horizontal forehead lines

Crow's Feet

Treatment of wrinkles around the eyes

Glabella

Smoothing of wrinkles between the eyebrows

Results gradually appear between 3-7 days and last 4-6 months. The procedure is quick, with minimal discomfort and no recovery time.
